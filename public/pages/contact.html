What exactly is BITE Club?

BITE Club is a nutritional science club that seeks to raise awareness of the nutritional value of the foods we consume daily. We do this through experiments in which foods are separated, fun demos in which we make yummy food, and themed food meetings in which we talk about the theme (i.e. vegan diets, non-GMOs) while eating delicious food.

Are there requirements for joining BITE Club?

Nah, just bring your appetite and interest in learning about food. There are no GPA or grade cutoffs, or science class requirement-- anyone who wants to join is free to do so!

Cool! How do I become a member?

You can fill out this form, or find one of the officers and give your contact information to them (name, email, grade).

Will there be tests and/or quizzes?

Nope!

How about lectures?

Only on occasion (to provide background knowledge about a future subject of discussion).
